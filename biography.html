Based in Madrid, we are a young duet formed by professional musicians with years of experience behind us, thus offering high quality performances and a variety of styles.
Our professional work has led us to travel through various parts of the world, from different cities in Latin America and North America to the Arab Emirates. We have also participated in concerts, recordings and festivals of different musical styles as well as recitals in various foundations.
We put at your disposal our experience, enthusiasm and enthusiasm to make your event unrepeatable, and what better way to do it than having the best music.
